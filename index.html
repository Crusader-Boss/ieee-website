Welcome To IEEE HIT SB

IEEE Student Branch of HIT Haldia seeks to enhance the learning experience of the student community on the HIT Haldia campus. The Student Branch focuses on conducting social and technical activities for students, and also encourages the students to take full advantage of the benefits of IEEE membership, including scholarships, competitions, and conference grants.
